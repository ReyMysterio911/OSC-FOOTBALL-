About Us








Kahikatea The Ormiston Senior College football team stands as a testament to the spirit of dedication, teamwork, and excellence that defines our school. Comprising talented and passionate players, our team is committed to achieving success on the field while fostering a culture of mutual respect and camaraderie. Our training sessions, led by experienced and dedicated coaches, focus on developing not only the technical and tactical skills of each player but also their physical fitness and mental resilience. We believe in the holistic development of our athletes, ensuring they grow both as players and individuals.
